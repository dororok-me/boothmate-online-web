How to request deletion: Send an email to dororok@gmail.com with the subject “Account deletion request” and the Google account email you use to sign in. We will confirm and complete the deletion within 7 business days.

Data deleted: account info (email, name), remaining time balance, cloud glossary, and usage history are permanently deleted. Payment records may be retained for up to 5 years as required by law. Voice audio is processed in real time and never stored, so there is no stored audio to delete.
